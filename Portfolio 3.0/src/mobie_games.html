About

Dice Fight is a turn-based strategy game for two to six players. The board is made up of randomly distributed hex tiles and represent territories that the players compete to conquer. Play begins with each player being given a complement of dice, which represent soldiers/armies on each tile. Each player's dice are distributed on his or her tiles on the board, with only one player being allowed to occupy any single given tile. During a player's turn, he or she may choose to use the dice from a territory to attack a neighboring territory. To resolve the attack, both the attacker and defender roll the dice present in the disputed territory and compare the result. The game is over when only one player's armies exist on the board or when one player controls all the king of the hill points until the beginning of their next turn.
